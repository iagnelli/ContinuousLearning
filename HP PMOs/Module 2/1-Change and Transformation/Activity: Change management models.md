## Scenario
A mid-sized technology company, TechInnovate, has decided to transform its PMO to become more agile, responsive, and integrated with its business strategy. The PMO has been in place for five years, providing governance, standardization, and oversight for all project activities. However, recent market pressures and rapid technological advancements require a transformation in how projects are managed to stay competitive. They have reached out and tasked you to guide them through the transformation.

## Scenario
TechInnovate is a large multinational company with operations in over 30 countries and a workforce of 50,000 employees. They are recognized for their cutting-edge products. However, in response to the rapidly changing technological landscape and increasing competition, the company has embarked on a massive digital transformation initiative. The transformation aims to modernize the company’s IT infrastructure, automate processes, enhance data analytics capabilities, and improve customer experience. 

TechInnovate has identified 120 projects as part of this digital transformation initiative. These projects range from upgrading legacy systems and implementing new enterprise resource planning (ERP) solutions to developing AI-driven products and enhancing cybersecurity measures. The projects vary in complexity, scope, and impact on the organization, with some being global in scale and others focused on specific regions or business units. 

Given the scale of this initiative, TechInnovate recognizes that it needs to establish a PMO maturity model and evaluate its PMO. They also want to use the Five Levels of Maturity Assessment to guide them through their digital transformation initiative. Given the company's scale, global presence, and complexity of projects, they want to ensure effective project management practices are in place.

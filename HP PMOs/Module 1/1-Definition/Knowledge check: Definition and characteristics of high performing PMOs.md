**Question 2**
What are the three primary types of PMOs?  

**Supportive PMO, Controlling PMO, and Directive PMO**  
Correct
That’s correct. The three primary types of PMOs are the Supportive PMO, which provides consultative support, templates, best practices, and training; the Controlling PMO, requiring compliance with specific project management methodologies and frameworks; and a Directive PMO, which directly manages and controls projects.

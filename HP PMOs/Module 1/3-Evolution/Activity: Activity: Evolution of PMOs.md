## Prerequisite
You will need a Microsoft account to access Microsoft Word. You will use the Word answer template, which you must download, to capture your answers for each step. 
Note that this activity is a practice exercise you must complete independently. It prepares you for the final project and assessment at the end of the professional program. You are not required to upload your answer document.

## Scenario
You have been appointed as TechInnovate's Program Manager to lead the transformation of its project management function from a traditional PMO to an SRO, 
with plans to eventually transition into a VRO. This transformation is crucial to ensuring that TechInnovate’s projects are aligned with its strategic goals 
and deliver measurable value as the company navigates a complex and competitive market environment.
Your task is to implement the necessary structures and processes that will guide this transformation, focusing on strategic alignment, 
continuous value delivery, and enhanced collaboration across departments.

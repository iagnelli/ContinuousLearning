## CFTE Payments Online Masterclass — Executive Summary

**ENGLISH**

The masterclass presents payments as one of the fastest-growing and most strategic areas in finance.
Payments are no longer only about cash, cards or bank transfers: they now include mobile payments, wallets, QR codes, contactless, account-to-account payments, crypto assets and **CBDCs (Central Bank Digital Currencies)**.
The key idea is that payments are the “Trojan horse” of finance: once a company controls the payment experience, it can expand into wallets, credit, loyalty, data services and broader financial ecosystems.
The sector is growing because customer behaviour has changed: people expect instant, simple, mobile and frictionless experiences.
Technology is reshaping the industry through mobile banking, e-commerce, cloud platforms, **IaC (Infrastructure as Code)**, containers and **PaaS (Payments as a Service)**.
Cross-border payments remain a major challenge because of old infrastructure, fragmented systems, **FX (Foreign Exchange)** complexity, fees, data management and trust issues.
Standards such as **ISO 20022 (International Organization for Standardization 20022)** can improve interoperability and reduce failed transactions.
Fintechs, card networks, banks and payment orchestrators are creating new value streams around payments, data, merchant services and platform-based models.
Overall, the course is useful as a high-level introduction, but it remains generic and should be complemented with deeper study on regulation, acceptance devices, standards, security and payment infrastructure.

**ITALIANO**

La masterclass presenta i pagamenti come una delle aree più dinamiche e strategiche della finanza.
I pagamenti non riguardano più solo contante, carte o bonifici: includono mobile payments, wallet, QR code, contactless, pagamenti account-to-account, crypto asset e **CBDC (Central Bank Digital Currencies)**.
L’idea centrale è che i pagamenti siano il “cavallo di Troia” della finanza: chi controlla l’esperienza di pagamento può poi espandersi verso wallet, credito, loyalty, dati e servizi finanziari più ampi.
La crescita è guidata dal cambiamento dei comportamenti dei clienti, che si aspettano esperienze immediate, semplici, mobili e senza attrito.
La tecnologia sta trasformando il settore tramite mobile banking, e-commerce, cloud, **IaC (Infrastructure as Code)**, container e **PaaS (Payments as a Service)**.
I pagamenti cross-border restano complessi per infrastrutture obsolete, sistemi frammentati, **FX (Foreign Exchange)**, costi, gestione dati e fiducia.
Standard come **ISO 20022 (International Organization for Standardization 20022)** possono migliorare interoperabilità ed efficienza.
Fintech, circuiti carta, banche e payment orchestrator stanno creando nuovi flussi di valore su pagamenti, dati, merchant services e modelli platform-based.
Nel complesso, il corso è utile come introduzione generale, ma va integrato con studio più profondo su normativa, dispositivi di accettazione, standard, sicurezza e infrastruttura dei pagamenti.

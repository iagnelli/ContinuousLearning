## Come convertire ADM comma 6b in “Amazon language”

Qui c’è il vero valore.

La tua esperienza non va detta come:
**“ho gestito compliance ADM”**

Va convertita così:

* **managed mission-critical regulated platforms**
* **balanced time-to-market, risk, and control requirements**
* **drove release/change governance with traceability and evidence**
* **partnered across tech, operations, compliance, and vendors**
* **used metrics and process controls to reduce incidents and audit findings**

Questa narrativa è forte sia per Campari sia per Amazon-style PM-T, perché combina **execution + governance + technical context**.

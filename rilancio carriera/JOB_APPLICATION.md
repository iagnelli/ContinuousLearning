# Head of Project Management

The Role
Condé Nast Italia is looking for a **Head of Project Management** with solid experience in the Creative Project Management of Digital, Social and Experiential assets. Maintain the interactions with clients and internal teams during all post-sale phases of projects
Complete supervision of production budgets and timelines, with direct responsibility for project P&L
Support to the Brand Marketing team in the preparation of final reports and case studies (custom and tentpole)
Ensure constant qualitative and quantitative alignment between commercial proposals and final deliverables

Primary Skills & Qualifications

7+ years of professional experience as a Lead Project Manager, Head of Project Management or Senior Project Manager in Media Companies, Creative Agencies, Event Management Companies or similar contexts
Proven experience in end-to-end production of creative and cross-platform content (videos, advertorials, branded content, social media campaigns, events, and in-event activations)
Professional working fluency in Italian and English, written and spoken
Experience in direct client management and supervision of productions, shoots, and events
Experience in team management, or proven strong and solution-oriented leadership attitude
